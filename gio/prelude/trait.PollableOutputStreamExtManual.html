Creates a glib::Source that triggers when self can be written, or cancellable is triggered or an error occurs. The callback on the source is of the GPollableSourceFunc type.

As with PollableOutputStream::is_writable, it is possible that the stream may not actually be writable even after the source triggers, so you should use PollableOutputStream::write_nonblocking rather than OutputStreamExt::write from the callback.
